Payment Information

Fees

Our school fees are structured to provide quality education while considering affordability for our students' families. The fees cover various aspects, including tuition, textbooks, extracurricular activities, and facility maintenance. Detailed information regarding the fees for each grade level can be obtained from the school administration office.

Payment Calendar

To ensure a smooth payment process, we have established a payment calendar indicating the due dates for fee payments. It is important to adhere to the payment deadlines to avoid any inconvenience. The payment calendar can be accessed on our website or obtained from the school administration office.

Outstanding Scholarship Program

As part of our commitment to academic excellence, we offer an outstanding scholarship program to recognize and support exceptional students. Scholarships are awarded based on academic achievements, extracurricular involvement, and community contributions. Eligible students can apply for scholarships during the designated application period. Detailed information about our scholarship program can be obtained from the school administration office.
